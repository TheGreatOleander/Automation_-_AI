Edge Functions, Webhooks, and Real-Time Transport

13.1 Cloudflare Workers for AI Automation

Cloudflare Workers run JavaScript/TypeScript at the network edge: 300+ data centres worldwide, sub-millisecond cold starts, and a generous free tier (100,000 requests/day). For AI automation, Workers serve as the always-on webhook receiver, the rate-limit enforcer, and the routing layer in front of your actual AI infrastructure.
